Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

At YUGAL Digital Tools, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality digital subscriptions and services. We understand that sometimes things don't go as planned, and we're here to help make it right.

24-Hour Return Policy

All our verified services come with a 24-hour return window. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase within the first 24 hours, we'll process your refund request.

Refund Policy

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

Eligible for Refund (Within 24 Hours Only):

  • Service not delivered after payment verification
  • Service not working as described on our website
  • Duplicate purchases made by mistake
  • Technical issues preventing service activation
  • Wrong product delivered

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Refund requests made after 24 hours of delivery
  • Change of mind after successful delivery
  • Violation of service provider's terms of service
  • Issues caused by incorrect information provided by customer
  • Services working as described but not meeting personal expectations

24-Hour Policy Strictly Enforced

Refund requests MUST be made within 24 hours of order delivery. No exceptions will be made after this period. Digital products are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned" after use.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Contact us via WhatsApp at +92 322 4083545 within 24 hours of delivery
  • Step 2: Provide your Order ID and payment screenshot
  • Step 3: Explain the issue you're experiencing with proof
  • Step 4: Our team will investigate within 2-4 hours
  • Step 5: If approved, refund will be processed within 24-48 hours

Refund Methods:

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during purchase:

  • Bank transfers: 5-7 business days
  • Mobile wallets (SadaPay, etc.): 2-3 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 1-2 business days
  • Card payments: 7-10 business days

Service Warranty

All our services come with a warranty period as specified on the product page:

  • During the warranty period, we'll replace any service that stops working
  • Warranty covers technical issues and service interruptions
  • Warranty does not cover account bans due to terms violation
  • Replacement is subject to availability of the service

Warranty Claims

To claim warranty, contact us immediately when you notice an issue. Provide your Order ID and a screenshot of the problem. We'll verify and provide a replacement within 24 hours.
